General FAQ’s

Frequently Asked Questions

  • 1. What types of mineral testing services does Zirconex Mine Labs offer?

    We offer world-class sampling and testing services, including spectrochemical analysis (XRF, ICP-OES, AAS), moisture and density testing, multi-element analysis, and ore beneficiation studies to ensure your minerals meet international quality standards.

  • 2. How does Zirconex ensure the accuracy of test results?

    Our labs follow rigorous QA/QC procedures, including chain-of-custody tracking, barcoding, and running blanks, duplicates, and certified reference materials to ensure result integrity and repeatability.

  • 3. Can Zirconex assist with mining license applications?

    Yes, we guide clients through the entire licensing process, including documentation, regulatory liaison, environmental compliance, and renewals, ensuring compliance with local and international regulations.

  • 4. How does Zirconex facilitate mineral exports?

    We provide export documentation, quality certifications, customs clearance, logistics coordination, and market intelligence to ensure smooth and compliant international trade of minerals.

  • 5. What is the turnaround time for test results?

    Our optimized workflows ensure fast turnarounds, with standard test results delivered promptly. Clients can track progress in real-time and download certified reports via our secure client portal.
