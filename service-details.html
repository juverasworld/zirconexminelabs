Sampling & Testing of Metals

We offer world-class metal sampling and testing services to guarantee that your mineral samples meet international quality standards before sale or processing.

Zirconex Mine Labs employs state-of-the-art laboratory equipment and rigorous QA/QC procedures to ensure accurate and reliable results. Our testing services are designed to support miners, investors, and traders in making informed decisions.

With facilities in Lagos and Abuja, we provide fast turnaround times and real-time tracking through our secure client portal, delivering certified reports that stand up to international scrutiny.

Our Process

Our metal sampling and testing process includes:

  • Sample Collection & Preparation: On-site sample collection, labeling, crushing, grinding, and homogenization for accurate lab analysis.
  • Spectrochemical Analysis: Using XRF, ICP-OES, and AAS techniques to determine metal content and trace elements.
  • Moisture & Density Testing: Critical for determining shipping weight and pricing of concentrates.
  • Compliance Reporting: Detailed test certificates formatted to meet local and international trade requirements.

Our multidisciplinary team of geologists, metallurgists, and laboratory scientists ensures precision at every stage of the testing process.

We prioritize confidentiality, security, and data integrity, with encrypted communications and tamper-evident certified reports available on request.

Frequently Asked Questions
