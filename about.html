About Zirconex

Who We Are

Zirconex Mine Labs is a pioneering mineral testing and mining solutions company, committed to raising the standard of mineral analysis, site assessments, and mining operations in Nigeria and across Africa. Founded just two years ago, we began with a simple mission: to provide miners, investors, and government agencies with reliable, world-class mineral testing services that drive informed decisions and maximize value across the mining value chain.
